# **App Name**: CapCut Mastery

## Core Features:

- Hero Section with Video: Displays a compelling hero section with title, subtitle, a 'Start Now' button, and space for embedding a YouTube video to capture initial user interest.
- Pain Point Cards: Presents three concise cards that highlight the target audience's problems related to video editing and content creation.
- Solution and Benefits Section: Explains the course's solutions and benefits, using icons to enhance readability and visual appeal, showcasing what students will learn.
- Price Highlight with CTA: Features a prominent display of the launch price (R$19.90) with a 'Buy Now' button linked to the provided checkout URL for immediate conversion.
- Testimonials Section: Includes three short testimonials with star ratings and illustrative photos to build trust and credibility.
- FAQ Section: Addresses common questions (4 Q&As) to alleviate concerns and provide essential information.
- Analytics Integration: Integrates Google Analytics and Meta Pixel support by allowing a script input, providing tracking for conversion optimization and audience analysis.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Gold (#fbbf24) for buttons to draw attention.
- Background color: Black (#000000) provides a sleek and modern aesthetic, setting a high-value tone.
- Accent color: White (#FFFFFF) used for text and sections to create contrast against the black background and improve readability. Sections will be in alternating black and white.
- Body and headline font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for a contemporary and precise look, ensuring legibility and a modern feel. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use rounded borders and soft shadows to give a modern and approachable feel.
- Ensure a fully responsive layout (mobile-first) to provide an optimal viewing experience on all devices.
- Subtle transitions and animations to improve user experience and engagement, e.g., hover effects on buttons.
